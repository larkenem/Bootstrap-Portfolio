About Me

Larken is a true North Carolina native, one of the last of her kind. Born and raised in a small town north of Boone, Larken grew up surrounded by mountains and the New River. Larken attended Appalachian State University and has lived in Charlotte since 2014.


Professionally, Larken has spent the last few years specializing in sales and sales management. As of 2018, she began her journey into the world of web development and computer programming.


In her free time, Larken enjoys spending time with her 8 year-old dog, Rona. She also enjoys exploring restaurants in the Charlotte area.


A proven fact about Larken is that she has never turned down a cup of coffee.
